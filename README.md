# thedetailers



Steps on iPhone Gmail app
Open Gmail app
Tap your profile picture top right
Tap Add another account
Choose Other (IMAP)
Enter your IONOS email, like info@yourdomain.com
Choose IMAP
Incoming server
Server: imap.ionos.com
Port: 993
Security: SSL/TLS
Username: your full email address
Password: your IONOS email password
Outgoing server
SMTP server: smtp.ionos.com
Port: 465
Security: SSL/TLS
Username: your full email address
Password: your IONOS email password
